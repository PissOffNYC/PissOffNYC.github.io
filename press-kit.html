PISS-OFF!

We are PISS-OFF! and we want to wet your pants and blow your mind. We are noisy, jumping, dancing, screaming, bouncing, hip-hop, punk, electronic, hardcore. We are the anti-band who often gets described in synomyms for loud. We rock rap shows and turn avante-garde dance performances into dirty raves.

PISS-OFF! is Brooklyn based rapper/singer/producer/artist Anika Trujillo and keyboardist/producer/vocalist/dj Gumball Eyeball. Our debut album, Perfect is for Amateurs, will be released this December 2015, with a release party at Palisades on December 6th.

Our latest video, ANTISOCIALITE, showcases our crazy antics.

Shows at: Santos Party House, Palisades, Tender Trap / Coco 66, Muchmores, Delancey, Rivington F&B, Bowery Electric, Don Pedro, Tarot Society, Bootleg Mansion
