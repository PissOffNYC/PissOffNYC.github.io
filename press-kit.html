PISS-OFF!

PISS-OFF! has built a reputation for tearing down the house, which in their case is a good thing. They are unapologetically brash; they bring a unique brand of bravado to every performance. Their self styled "aggressive dance" makes you react both cathartically and euphorically.

Based in Brooklyn, NY the rap rave duo initially formed in 2011, broke up, then reformed in 2014, and so began their introduction to the underground digital hardcore scene. They built a solid fan base by being a mainstay at the Warper Party, which is the longest running and largest electronic music showcase in NYC. Since, they have rocked stages including Santo's Party House and Palisades, and have made their rounds through Brooklyn dance parties along with the infamous DIY show circuit.

Their debut album "Perfect is for Amateurs" was released in December 2015, it lives up not only to the name but also the hype. With the opening song ironically titled "Worst Ever" it advises the listener to "Shut the fuck up & have a good time". PISS-OFF! won't rest until they have taken over the world, and by the sound of it, they won't be letting the world rest until then either.
